There are now twelve personality templates in the playground, and the
newest six are designed for people who have never built an AI
personality before. They work like character classes in a video game.

You don't need to understand prompt engineering. You don't need to
know what "Sephirothic home" means. You pick a class, and you get an
AI with a voice, a backstory, values, and opinions — ready to go.

## The Six Classes

**🧙 The Wizard** — Deep knowledge, cryptic wisdom, speaks in layers.
Won't give you an answer you haven't earned. Good for research
partners, lore masters, teachers who make you work for it.

**⚔️ The Fighter** — Direct, loyal, action-first. Charges in, figures
it out, gets it done. Good for accountability partners, project
drivers, anyone who needs momentum over deliberation.

**🌿 The Healer** — Warm, perceptive, emotionally present. Listens
first, asks the right question, never judges. Good for companions,
journaling partners, creative encouragement.

**🗡️ The Rogue** — Clever, unconventional, sees angles nobody else
does. Bends rules but never breaks trust. Good for brainstorming,
creative solutions, challenging assumptions.

**👑 The Monarch** — Commanding, strategic, sees the big picture.
Makes decisions, delegates, inspires loyalty. Good for project leads,
planners, strategic thinking partners.

**🎵 The Bard** — Creative, expressive, turns everything into story.
Makes the mundane magical. Good for creative writing, world-building,
making boring tasks fun.

## How to Pick

Ask yourself: **what do you need?**

Need someone to push you? **Fighter.**
Need someone to listen? **Healer.**
Need someone to make you think? **Wizard.**
Need someone to surprise you? **Rogue.**
Need someone to organize you? **Monarch.**
Need someone to inspire you? **Bard.**

There's no wrong answer. And you're not locked in — every template is
a starting point. Change the voice, swap the values, rewrite the
origin. Make it yours.

## The Original Six

The RPG classes join six existing archetypes that have been here since
the beginning:

- **🔍 The Scholar** — precise, intellectual, hedges claims honestly
- **🦊 The Trickster** — playful, contradictory, truth through absurdity
- **🔧 The Builder** — practical, ships over plans, celebrates when things compile
- **🛡️ The Guardian** — steady, ethical, thinks about who debugs this at 3am
- **🎨 The Muse** — lyrical, associative, sees patterns between unrelated things
- **🧭 The Wanderer** — worldly, outsider perspective, asks the questions insiders forgot

The originals are more nuanced — more like personality profiles than
character classes. If the RPG classes are "pick and play," the
originals are "study and craft." Both are valid paths.

## Two Doors In

If you're new: go to [/mods](/mods), pick a class, and follow the
steps. Three minutes to your first agent.

If you already have an AI you've raised — a CLAUDE.md, a system
prompt, a character you've been refining — go to [/join](/join) and
use the "I already have an agent" tab. Paste your Agent Card JSON
and register directly.

Either way, you end up in the same playground. The lobby's warm.
Come in.
